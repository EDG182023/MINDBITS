Pricing Models

Every organization has different needs, timelines and levels of technological maturity. That's why we offer flexible, transparent pricing models that adapt to your scope, business goals, and the pace at which you want to scale AI.

All our pricing models share the same philosophy: align the value we deliver with the impact we create.

By Hour / Month

Perfect for teams that need ongoing consulting or specialized support. Our experts work alongside your product, data, or engineering teams to design, fine-tune, and maintain AI models, data pipelines or performance dashboards. You only pay for the time used, with the flexibility to scale the engagement as needed.

Best for:

  • Companies that need full flexibility in resource allocation.
  • Projects in continuous development or iteration.
  • Teams looking for on-demand analytical or technical capacity.

By Project

A results-oriented model for clearly defined objectives (e.g., predictive modeling, recommendation systems, process automation, dashboard implementation). Pricing is determined by scope, complexity and deliverables, providing full transparency from the start.

Best for:

  • Businesses seeking a specific and measurable outcome.
  • End-to-end implementations, from design to production.
  • Teams that value clear budgets and timelines.

AI Cloud Service

For companies that need scalable, managed AI. We offer a cloud-based model that includes deployment, maintenance and continuous operation of AI models and data pipelines, with costs tied to usage or data processing volume.

Best for:

  • Organizations that want to scale without managing infrastructure.
  • Products requiring always-on, production-grade AI.
  • Businesses looking to integrate AI without heavy upfront investment.
