Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Mindbits.ai provide?

We specialize in data analytics, artificial intelligence and machine learning consulting. Core services include:

  • Predictive and behavioral modeling.
  • Product and monetization analytics.
  • Data architecture and dashboarding.
  • Pricing optimization and forecasting.
  • AI integration and automation.

We help companies turn raw data into actionable strategies that drive measurable outcomes.

What industries do you work with?

We work across multiple industries, with deepest expertise in gaming, from mobile and console to live-service titles. We also collaborate with tech startups, SaaS companies and AI-driven businesses that want to scale their data and analytics.

How does Mindbits.ai structure its engagements?

We offer three flexible pricing models:

  • By Hour/Month: Perfect for ongoing consulting and specialized support.
  • By Project: Results-oriented for clearly defined objectives.
  • AI Cloud Service: Scalable, managed AI solutions with usage-based pricing.

All models align value with impact and provide transparent pricing.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope:

  • Quick consultations: 2-4 weeks
  • Predictive models: 6-12 weeks
  • Full analytics platforms: 3-6 months
  • AI integration projects: 8-16 weeks

We always provide detailed project plans with clear milestones and deliverables.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?

Security and compliance are fundamental to our process:

  • End-to-end encryption and secure data handling
  • GDPR and industry-specific compliance frameworks
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Comprehensive data governance policies
  • Secure development environments and deployment protocols

We sign NDAs and provide full documentation of our security measures.

What kind of support do you provide post-launch?

We provide comprehensive post-launch support:

  • 24/7 monitoring and alerting systems
  • Regular performance optimization and updates
  • Dedicated support channels and response teams
  • Documentation and training for your team
  • Continuous improvement based on user feedback

Our goal is to ensure long-term success and ROI from your AI investments.
