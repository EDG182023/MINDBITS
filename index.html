From Hypothesis to Proven Growth

We help you replace guesswork with measurable impact. Mindbits.ai designs experimentation programs and robust attribution so your team learns fast, scales what works, and stops what doesn't.

A/B Testing as a System (not a one-off)

We build an experimentation engine your product and marketing teams can run weekly:

  • Hypothesis → Design → Power: test plans with effect size targets, sample size, and power calculations.
  • Guardrails: metrics for revenue, retention, latency, crashes, and fraud, so wins don't hide trade-offs.
  • Statistical rigor: sequential testing, CUPED/variance reduction, and non-parametric tests when needed.
  • Automation: experiment registry, auto-exposure, and pipelines for lift computation and reporting.

User Acquisition you can actually trust

We reconcile acquisition spend with real business outcomes:

  • Attribution you can defend: SKAN/SKAdNetwork workflows, MMP integration, server-side postbacks, and MMM/MTA when applicable.
  • Incrementality over vanity: geo-split tests, PSA/ghost ads, and holdouts to quantify true lift.
  • Creative & audience feedback loops: rapid creative iteration, audience pruning, and budget reallocation based on causal impact.
  • Fraud & quality controls: detection of click spam/device farms, uninstall/retention filters, and cohort quality scoring.

Retention & Engagement Models

Growth doesn't stop at acquisition. We model what keeps users coming back:

  • Churn prediction: identify at-risk users before they leave.
  • Cohort analysis: retention curves by acquisition source, feature usage, and time.
  • Engagement scoring: quantify user health and trigger interventions.
  • Feature adoption: which features drive stickiness, which are ignored.

Revenue & Monetization Analytics

From LTV to pricing experiments, we build the analytics layer for revenue optimization:

  • LTV modeling: predictive models for customer lifetime value by cohort.
  • Pricing elasticity: how does price impact conversion and revenue?
  • Subscription metrics: MRR, ARR, churn, expansion revenue, and renewal rates.
  • Revenue attribution: tie revenue back to campaigns, features, and product changes.
